# SoulSpeak Design System

## Colors (SSColors)
- **Primary**: Deep purple (#6B4EAB) - main interactive elements
- **Secondary**: Lighter purple (#9B7ED8) - secondary actions
- **Accent**: Warm gold - highlights and awards
- **Background**: Light lavender (#F8F6FF) - app background
- **Surface**: White - cards and elevated surfaces
- **Semantic Colors**: success (green), warning (orange), error (red)
- **Mood Colors**: calm (blue), energy (orange), growth (green), love (pink)

## Typography (SSTypography)
- **System Design**: SF Rounded for UI, SF Serif for journal content
- **Scale**: largeTitle, title, title2, title3, headline, body, callout, caption, caption2
- **Special**: journalBody (serif), affirmation (italic rounded), quote (serif italic)

## Components
- **SSButton**: Primary, secondary, outline, ghost, destructive variants
- **SSCard**: Elevated surface with shadow and rounded corners
- **SSGradientCard**: Card with gradient background for emphasis

## Spacing
- Base unit: 4px
- Component padding: 16px
- Section spacing: 20px
- Screen padding: 16-24px

## Corner Radius
- Small (chips): 8px
- Medium (buttons): 12px
- Large (cards): 16px
- Full (avatars): 50%
