# SoulSpeak - App Store Listing

## App Name
SoulSpeak - Mindful Journal

## Subtitle
Private Wellness & Mood Companion

## Description
SoulSpeak is your private space for self-reflection, emotional growth, and inner peace. Built with privacy as the foundation, every thought you share stays on your device — encrypted, protected, and truly yours.

**Journal Your Journey**
Write freely with guided prompts, mood tagging, and optional AES-256 encryption. Your thoughts deserve the highest level of protection.

**Track Your Mood**
Understand your emotional patterns with visual mood trends, trigger identification, and energy tracking. Knowledge is the first step to growth.

**Daily Affirmations**
Start each day with carefully curated affirmations or create your own. Build a collection that speaks to your soul.

**Breathe & Be Present**
Guided breathwork exercises help you find calm in moments of stress. Choose from Box Breathing, 4-7-8, and energizing techniques.

**Practice Gratitude**
Cultivate positivity with daily gratitude journaling. Watch your streak grow as thankfulness becomes a habit.

**Build Meaningful Rituals**
Create personalized daily rituals that nurture your well-being. Track completion and build consistency.

**Celebrate Growth**
Set milestones, earn achievements, and visualize your progress. Every step forward matters.

**Privacy Promise**
- All data stored locally on your device
- Face ID / Touch ID protection
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive entries
- Zero analytics or tracking
- No account required
- No data ever leaves your device

**Crisis Support**
If you're going through a difficult time, SoulSpeak provides immediate access to crisis resources including the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
